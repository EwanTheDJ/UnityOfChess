Introducing The Unity Of Chess!

Our latest game from us! The Unity Of Chess is developed on the unity engine, hence the name, and is a game aimed for players who like the accesibility of being able to play chess on PC without the need of setting up any physical pieces on boards or finding lost pieces!

A whole different way to play a classic game!

The Unity Of Chess loads up the board so you don't have to! As soon as you load this game up, you can get straight to playing some chess!

No need to remember the moves!

The Unity Of Chess knows the moves for each piece when you click on them, so that means less time remembering moves and a faster way to beat your opponent!
